# Training protocol

## Water restriction
For the duration of the experiment mice will be placed on water restriction so that we can give the mice water rewards as a reinforcer to learn the task.

## Habituation 
Habituation to the mouse handler:
Day 1: Put hand in cage  - let them get used to your scent
Day 2: Gently pick them up in tube and then place the tube down in the cage 
Day 3: Allow them to walk out of the tube into your palm
